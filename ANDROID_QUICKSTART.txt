Android-Only Quickstart (No PC)
===============================
1) Export APK (APK Exporter).
2) Open APK in MT Manager -> assets/bin/Data -> extract *.assets, *.resource, *.resS, globalgamemanagers, level*.
3) Try "Assets Browser" inside MT Manager to view textures & audio; export what you can.
4) Place exports into this template by category.
5) Use ZArchiver to compress the main folder into a single ZIP to share or back up.
